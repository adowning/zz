PAYTABLE

PAYOUTS

  • To obtain coins a player should shoot an animal and other objects. In order to start shooting, a player should click on the chosen animal.
  • When a bullet is fired into empty space and reached a game window borders, it will ricochet. If it doesn’t reach any animal/object at the first bounce, it will ricochet again and keep doing so until it reaches any.
  • This fixed odds game is a game of chance type of game, where the players' abilities or skills do not influence the game.
  • The maximum win in the game has an upper limit. For more information see the TERMS AND CONDITIONS section.

ANIMAL SEASONS

  • Animal Season triggers a period of increased win probabilities
  • When returning to the game after getting disconnected during an animal season, the remaining increased win probabilities at the time of disconnection are restored
  • Animal seasons may be triggered randomly after any shot at any animal, object or by killing the Bomb.
  • Note that Bomb and Dragon do not appear during Animal Season feature.

DISCONNECTION POLICY

NOTE ON DISCONNECTION

  • If you are disconnected from the Internet in the middle of the game, log in to the casino again. Once you have reopened the game, you will be able to continue playing from the point where the game was disconnected.
